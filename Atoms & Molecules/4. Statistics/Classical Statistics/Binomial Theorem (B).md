# Overview

The binomial theorem is the expansion of a binomial expression raised to a finite power: $(x+y)^n$. It is $$(x+y)^n=\sum_{k=0}^n\binom{n}{k}x^{n-k}y^k$$
Note $B(N,0)=1$, there is 1 way to choose 0 objects from $N$ objects.
