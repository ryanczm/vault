# Moments

The $n^{th}$ moment of $X$ is $E(X^n)$, central moment is $E[(X-\mu)^n]$ and standardized moment is $E[((X-\mu)/\sigma)^n]$. 

# Moment Generating Functions

The moment generating function is defined as $M_X(t)=E[e^{tX}]$.
